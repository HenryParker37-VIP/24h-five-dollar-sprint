# Buyer Flow

## Before payment

Buyer sees:

- $5 price.
- What they get.
- 24-hour manual delivery.
- No follower, sales, or income guarantee.
- No password needed.

## Payment

Buyer pays through public PayPal link.

If PayPal link is not set, do not publish the page yet. Replace `PAYPAL_LINK_HERE` first.

## Intake

Ask buyer for:

- Profile link or screenshots.
- Platform.
- Main goal.
- Target audience.
- Words or claims to avoid.
- PayPal screenshot or transaction email.

## Verification

Check PayPal manually:

- Amount is $5 USD.
- Name/email or transaction matches buyer.
- Payment is completed.

Do not ask for private PayPal login details.

## Fulfillment

Use:

- `product/bio-fix-kit.md`
- `product/delivery-template.txt`
- `product/example-delivery.md`

Send the result within 24 hours.

## After delivery

Ask:

```text
Did one of these feel useful enough to paste? If yes, I’d appreciate a quick reply. If not, tell me what tone you want and I’ll adjust once.
```

One small revision is fair for a $5 offer. Avoid unlimited revisions.
