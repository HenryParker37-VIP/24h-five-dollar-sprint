# 24-Hour Execution Checklist

## Hour 0-1

- [ ] Replace `PAYPAL_LINK_HERE` in `landing-page/index.html`.
- [ ] Open the landing page locally.
- [ ] Click the PayPal button and confirm it goes to the right public payment page.
- [ ] Add your contact method to the landing page if you want one visible.
- [ ] Publish or share the landing page link.

## Hour 1-3

- [ ] Publish the pinned post announcement on your strongest personal channel.
- [ ] Post 1 Vietnamese version if your audience uses Vietnamese.
- [ ] Post 1 English version if your audience uses English.
- [ ] Ask 5 warm contacts if they know a creator/freelancer who wants a $5 profile cleanup.

## Hour 3-8

- [ ] Find 10 profiles with unclear bios.
- [ ] Write one personal note for each.
- [ ] Send only approved, customized DMs.
- [ ] Leave 3 helpful comments in relevant threads without spamming links.
- [ ] Track every action in `ops/tracking.md`.

## Hour 8-16

- [ ] Follow up with people who replied.
- [ ] Offer a tiny free tip before asking for payment.
- [ ] When someone pays, collect intake info.
- [ ] Deliver the first order as fast as possible.

## Hour 16-24

- [ ] Post proof of process without exposing buyer private info.
- [ ] Ask the buyer for permission to share a before/after.
- [ ] If no sale yet, offer 2 live mini-critiques publicly and link the $5 service after.
- [ ] Keep outreach personal and ethical.

## First buyer sprint

- [ ] Warm audience post.
- [ ] 10 personalized messages.
- [ ] 3 useful comments.
- [ ] 1 public before/after using the sample profile.
- [ ] 1 follow-up post after 6 hours.
