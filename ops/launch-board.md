# First $5 Launch Board

Use this when you are ready to execute. Do not overthink the funnel.

## Step 1: Make the page payable

- Replace `PAYPAL_LINK_HERE` in `landing-page/index.html`.
- Add your preferred contact handle to the page or to every post.
- Open the page locally and click the payment button.

## Step 2: Publish one warm post

Post this first on your strongest personal channel:

```text
I’m doing a tiny 24-hour experiment: $5 profile bio fixes for creators/freelancers. Send a profile link, I’ll send 3 clearer bio options, 3 pinned post hooks, and 5 notes. No hype, just cleaner words.
```

## Step 3: Message 10 warm or relevant people

Send only personalized messages. Best targets:

1. A friend with a small creator page.
2. A classmate building a portfolio.
3. A freelancer with a vague LinkedIn headline.
4. A small Instagram shop with unclear product text.
5. A TikTok creator whose bio does not name the audience.
6. A YouTuber with no clear pinned intro.
7. A student looking for internships.
8. A coach or tutor with a broad bio.
9. A maker building in public.
10. Someone who recently asked for profile or content feedback.

DM:

```text
Hey, quick note: I saw your profile and the content looks interesting, but the bio could be clearer about who it is for. I’m doing a tiny $5 bio fix today: 3 rewrite options + 3 pinned hooks + 5 notes. Want me to send the details?
```

Before asking for payment, give a tiny useful observation:

```text

```

Then, if they are interested:

