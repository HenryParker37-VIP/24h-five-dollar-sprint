# Where To Promote Today

Read community rules first. If self-promotion is banned, do not post a sales link. Contribute useful advice instead.

## First 10 places / people / community types

1. Your own Facebook profile or story.
2. Your own Instagram story.
3. Your own X/Twitter profile.
4. Creator or small business Facebook groups that allow offer posts.
5. Reddit threads where people ask for profile feedback.
6. Indie maker communities with feedback channels.
7. Discord servers with creator-feedback or self-promo channels.
8. Friends or classmates with small creator pages.
9. Local freelancers or student creators whose bio is vague.
10. Small shops on Instagram that have unclear product descriptions.

## Search phrases

- "profile feedback"
- "bio feedback"
- "roast my landing page"
- "new creator advice"
- "how to get clients from LinkedIn"
- "small business Instagram bio"
- "TikTok bio help"
- "creator feedback thread"

## Priority order

1. Warm contacts first.
2. Your own public post second.
3. Helpful comments third.
4. Carefully personalized DMs fourth.

## Avoid

- Mass DMs.
- Posting the same text everywhere.
- Groups that ban promotion.
- Claims like "guaranteed sales" or "grow fast".
