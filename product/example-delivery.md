# Example Delivery

## Buyer

A small fitness creator whose current bio says:

```text
Fitness lover. Helping people be better. DM me.
```

## Quick diagnosis

Your current bio has good energy, but it is too broad. A new visitor cannot tell who you help, what result you focus on, or why they should DM you. The fastest fix is to name the audience and make the CTA more specific.

## Bio option 1 - Clear

```text
Simple home workouts for busy beginners.
Build strength without gym stress.
DM "START" for a 10-min plan.
```

## Bio option 2 - Warm

```text
Helping busy beginners feel stronger at home.
No gym pressure. No perfect routine needed.
DM "START".
```

## Bio option 3 - Sales-focused

```text
Home fitness plans for busy beginners.
10-minute workouts, simple habits, real consistency.
DM "START" for help.
```

## Pinned post hooks

1. Start here if you want to get fit but hate complicated workout plans.
2. I help busy beginners build a home workout habit in 10 minutes a day.
3. 3 beginner fitness mistakes that make consistency harder than it needs to be.

## 5 quick profile notes

1. Replace "fitness lover" with who you help.
2. Use one CTA instead of a general "DM me".
3. Mention "home workouts" if that is your main content.
4. Pin one post that explains your method.
5. Add one proof point later if you have it, such as your own transformation or client result.

## Best version to paste today

```text
Simple home workouts for busy beginners.
Build strength without gym stress.
DM "START" for a 10-min plan.
```

## Why this one

It is specific, friendly, and gives visitors one clear action.
