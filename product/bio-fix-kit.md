Send one concise document with:

1. Quick diagnosis.
2. 3 bio options.
3. 3 pinned post or headline hooks.
4. 5 profile improvement notes.
5. Best final version to paste.
6. Optional next step.

## The 7-minute rewrite method

### 1. Identify the profile job

Choose one:

- Get followers.
- Get DMs.
- Get buyers.
- Get clients.
- Get newsletter signups.
- Build credibility.

### 2. Extract raw material

Look for:

- Who they help.
- What outcome they create.
- Proof, niche, identity, location, style, or product.
- Current CTA.
- Confusing words to remove.

### 3. Use the bio formula

```text
I help [specific person] get [specific outcome] with [method/style/product].
[Proof or personality signal].
[Simple CTA].
```

Shorter version:

```text
[Identity] for [audience].
[Outcome or content promise].
[CTA].
```

### 4. Write three angles

- Clear: says exactly what they do.
- Warm: more human and casual.
- Sales: stronger call-to-action.

### 5. Write pinned hooks

Good pinned hooks:

- "Start here if..."
- "I help X do Y without Z."
- "3 things I wish I knew before..."
- "I rebuilt my profile so people understand..."
- "New here? Here's what I do..."

### 6. Give five notes

Keep notes practical:

- Remove vague claims.
- Put audience first.
- Add one proof point.
- Use one CTA.
- Match profile photo, banner, and pinned post.

### 7. End with a paste-ready version

Make the final recommendation obvious so the buyer can use it immediately.

## Delivery quality checklist

- No fake claims.
- No income promises.
- No made-up credentials.
- No shaming the buyer.
- Keep it encouraging and clear.
- Keep bios short enough for the platform.
- Give at least one option that fits their current voice.
