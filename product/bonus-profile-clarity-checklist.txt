Bonus: 5-Minute Profile Clarity Checklist

Give this to every buyer with their delivery.

1. The 5-second test
Ask someone to look at your profile for 5 seconds, then answer:
- Who is this for?
- What do they post or sell?
- What should I do next?

2. Bio formula
I help [specific person] get [specific result] with [method].
[Simple proof or personality signal].
[One CTA].

Or:
[What you make/do] for [specific audience].
[Why it matters].
[One CTA].

3. Remove vague words
Replace words like helping people, grow, level up, success, lifestyle, and value with specific words like home workouts for busy beginners, short videos for local shops, Notion systems for students, or product photos for handmade sellers.

4. Use one CTA
Pick one action: follow, DM, book, read the pinned post, or shop the link. Do not ask for three actions at once.

5. Pin one starter post
Good formats:
- Start here if you are new.
- I help [audience] do [result].
- 3 mistakes I keep seeing in [niche].
- My simple method for [result].
- What I would do first if I were [audience].

6. Match the profile
Your bio, profile photo, banner, and pinned post should all point to the same idea.

7. Do not invent proof
Use real proof only. No fake testimonials, fake numbers, or income claims.
