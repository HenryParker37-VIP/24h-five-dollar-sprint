# Quick Market Research Notes

Date: 2026-06-26

## What showed up

- Free or no-monthly-fee platforms are commonly recommended for tiny digital products: Payhip, Ko-fi, Gumroad, and simple static pages.
- Reddit discussions around digital products repeatedly warn that direct link dumping gets ignored or banned. Helpful comments and relevant conversations are safer.
- Low-cost products can sell, but generic prompt bundles are crowded.
- A service-shaped $5 offer can be easier to sell in 24 hours because the buyer gets personal attention instead of another static file.

## Chosen offer

**$5 Bio Fix + Pinned Hook Pack**

## Why this over a generic prompt bundle

- Easier to personalize in a DM or comment.
- Buyer can understand the value in one sentence.
- Delivery is fast and manual, so no automation needs to work before the first sale.
- The result is tangible: 3 bios, 3 hooks, 5 notes.
- It avoids fake income claims and does not require paid tools.
